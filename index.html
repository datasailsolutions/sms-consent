SMS Consent Policy

Your Consent

Submitting your phone number is completely optional to use our service. It only indicates your consent to receive occasional SMS messages from Datasail Solutions. These messages may include updates, reminders, or follow-up requests related to your account or communication with us.

Opt-Out Anytime

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying with "STOP" or "99" to any message. Once you opt out, we will stop sending SMS to your number immediately.

Optional Participation

You may continue to use our services without providing a phone number or receiving text messages. Participation in SMS communication is entirely at your discretion.
